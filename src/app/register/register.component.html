Registration Fee Structure

  • To participate in the conference every one should register first
  • Since the conference is going to held virtually there is no concession fee or member fee
  • If you are an Oral Presenter or as a Poster presenter you should register as a delegate/student.
  • If you are a listener you should register as an attendee.
  • The registration fee is based on your citizenship.
  • The postdoc will not be considered as a student.

Registration Fee Structure

  • The fee will be collected through online banking
  • The banking details and registration form will be updated soon
  • The fee structure is divided into four slabs
  • The fee for the slab 1 is a reduced fee for the first 25 registration (1-25) in each category
  • The fee will be increased for slab 2 for the next 25 registration (26-50) in each category
  • Similarly, the fee will be increased for slab 3 and slab 4
  • Number registration will be updated regularly at 11 pm once the registration is open
