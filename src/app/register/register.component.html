Registration Fee Structure

  • To participate in the conference every one must register and pay the registration fee.
  • SIf you are a listener you should register as a participant.
  • if you are a presenter (Oral/Poster) you should register as a presenting.
  • The registration fee is based on your citizenship.
  • The postdoc will not be considered as a student.
  • E-certificate will be provided to all the conference participants and presenters.

FEE GUIDELINES

  • The fee will be collected through online banking
  • The banking details and registration form will be updated soon
  • The fee structure is divided into three categories
    • Early bird
    • Regular
    • Conference Day
