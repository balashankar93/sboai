We are pleased to announce that “30th ANNUAL SBAOI MEETING & INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON BIOMEDICAL MATERIALS INNOVATION-2020 (ICBMI-2020)” will be held from 6/12/2020 to 9/12/2020

Innovations in Biomedical Materials Reasearch Challenges faced by our society in pursuing innovative research in biomedical materials with the help of emerging technologies to create futuristic hybrid therapeutic products and functionalities.

We request the honour of your presence at the inaugural ceremony of ICBMI-2020 on 06 Dec 2020 at 18:00 hrs IST and C. P. Sharma award plenary lecture at 18:30 hrs IST. The Conference will take place in the virtual environment due to the unforeseen COVID-19 pandemic. We look forward to welcoming you.
